Menwa ITB - Yonif 300/Raider

2015

Rappelling Helicopter Maybe one of the unforgettable training I've ever had. How did I get into this kind of stuff? Well you may not know but in Indonesian universities, we have extra optional activities provided by the campus. One of them is called resimen mahasiswa (student regiment), abbreviated as Menwa, where we get military dicipline training from the Indonesian Army. Long story short, we need to perform on the yearly welcoming event of new students. That year, we decided rapelling from helicopter is a cool stuff that can make them interested in joining us. So yeah, we contact and ask permissions here and there, do all the procedure and administrative work to get permitted to this training. You can watch the original video here or below. Go to min 2:15 for the helicopter rappelling part.

Menwa ITB - Pusdikif TNI AD

2013

Trainer Course (Suspelat/Kursus Pelatih) Training to be a trainer. As I mentioned above, Menwa ITB requires some phyisical and mental condition. Not everyone who sign up to join Menwa got accepted. They got tested and trained first, and the gate they have to pass is the Basic Education and Training (Diklatsar/Pendidikan dan Latihan Dasar). So this training is to prepare me to be the trainer in Diklatsar. Here, I was taught about psychology of teaching and giving orders and basic medical treatment.

Menwa ITB - Dodik Bela Negara TNI AD

Basic Education and Training (Diklatsar/Pendidikan dan Latihan Dasar) The gate training for entering Menwa.
